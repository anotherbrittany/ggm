So what's a “Girl Gone Mild”?

Girls Gone Mild was founded in 2012 by a group of ladies who were tired of the mainstream media’s female stereotypes. We believe women are beautiful, hilarious, intelligent, strong, classy, and not defined by what we wear, how we look, how much we weigh, or who we are (or aren't) dating. We enjoy the occasional glass of wine, but we're honestly more comfortable reading a good book than dancing at a club on a Saturday night.

As we traveled the world, we began meeting other women of all walks of life who seemed relieved to find other women who shared their views and interests.

We knit. We read. We run. We laugh. We dream. We create.

We are Girls Gone Mild.
